In-person workshop

An in-person workshop will be offered to EOSS Wellcome and Kavli grantees in late 2025 or early 2026. Travel and accommodation expenses will be covered for 1 participant from each grantee team. A hybrid workshop for all EOSS grantees will be offered in June 2025.

Asynchronous resources

Asynchronous resources and support. Grantees will have access to a bank of readings, templates, prototypes, examples, and other resources.
